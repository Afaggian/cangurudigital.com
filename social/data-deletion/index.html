Canguru Social

Data Deletion Instructions

How to Request Data Deletion

Canguru Social is an internal social media management tool used by Canguru Digital to manage its own Facebook and Instagram pages. We do not collect or store personal data from external users.

What Data We Store

  • Page Access Tokens for our own Facebook/Instagram pages
  • Scheduled post content (captions, image URLs, scheduling times)

Requesting Deletion

If you wish to request deletion of any data associated with your Facebook account, please contact us:

  • Email: alex@cangurudigital.com

We will process your request within 30 days and confirm deletion via email.

Automatic Removal

You can also remove our app's access to your Facebook account at any time by visiting your Facebook Business Integrations settings and removing "Canguru Social Poster".
